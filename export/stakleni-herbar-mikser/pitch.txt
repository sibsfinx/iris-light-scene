STAKLENI HERBAR / GLASS HERBARIUM
Mikser Festival — open call submission
—————————————————————————————————————

PROJECT TITLE
Stakleni herbar (Glass Herbarium)

PITCH
Iris reichenbachii still grows on Zlatibor — protected on paper,
invisible in public consciousness. I rendered it as crystal glass:
a real-time 3D environment for projection and a print series,
because that's what our relationship with these species actually
looks like — beautiful, untouchable, already half-gone.

FORMAT
Projection / screen — real-time 3D environment running live,
looping, ambient. Viewers can interact (orbit, zoom) or it plays
as an autonomous slow rotation. Scales to any surface from a
single monitor to a full wall projection.

Print series (support) — 4–6 large-format prints, different angles
and lighting. Can include minimal botanical annotation (Latin name,
habitat, protection status) as a quiet counter to the visual
abstraction.

SPECIES
Iris reichenbachii — Zlatibor mountain iris, protected under
Serbian law (Pravilnik o proglašavanju divljih vrsta).
First species in what is intended as an ongoing series covering
Serbian endemic and endangered flora: Picea omorika (Pančić's
spruce, Drina canyon), Ramonda nathaliae (Natalija flower,
Serbia's national endemic).

TECHNICAL
Real-time 3D (WebGL / Three.js), runs in browser, no installation.
Projection-ready: any aspect ratio, any resolution up to 4K.
HD PNG export built in.
